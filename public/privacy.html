1. Information we collect

We collect the following categories of information:

Account information

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and authentication credentials (such as a hashed password or a token from a third-party sign-in provider).

Content you upload

The Service is designed to process the call recordings, audio files, transcripts, and notes that you choose to import ("Content"). This Content may contain personal information about you and about third parties you speak with — for example, clients, prospects, and other contacts.

Information extracted from your Content

SonarPilot automatically analyzes your Content to extract and organize structured information, which may include:

  • Contacts — names, phone numbers, email addresses, and roles;
  • Properties and deals — addresses, budgets, price points, and deal stage;
  • Follow-up tasks — action items and due dates;
  • Meetings — proposed dates, times, and descriptions.

Usage and device information

We collect basic technical information automatically, such as device and browser type, operating system, IP address, approximate location derived from IP address, pages or screens viewed, and dates and times of access. We use this to operate, secure, and improve the Service.

Cookies and local storage

We use cookies and similar technologies (including browser local storage) to keep you signed in, remember your preferences, and understand how the Service is used. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling them may affect functionality.

2. Call recordings & consent

You are responsible for recording lawfully. Illinois is an "all-party consent" state under the Illinois Eavesdropping Act (720 ILCS 5/14), which generally requires the consent of all parties before a private conversation is recorded. Other states and countries have their own recording laws.

By importing a recording or transcript into SonarPilot, you represent and warrant that you obtained any consents required by applicable law to record the conversation and to process it using the Service. SonarPilot does not record calls for you and is not responsible for how you obtained your Content.

Biometric information. SonarPilot does not collect, capture, or store "biometric identifiers" or "biometric information" as those terms are defined in the Illinois Biometric Information Privacy Act (BIPA, 740 ILCS 14). We do not use voiceprints or facial recognition. We process audio to produce text and extract the structured fields described above — not to identify individuals by physiological characteristics.

These providers process your Content only to perform services for us, are bound by confidentiality and data-protection obligations, and are not permitted to use your Content for their own purposes, including model training.

Automated extraction is provided "as is" and may contain errors. You remain responsible for reviewing extracted information before relying on it.
